About Me

I'm a student designer who loves turning simple ideas into soft, modern interfaces inspired by portfolios on Dribbble and Figma Community.

I'm Judith Kim Marie Marbascias, a 3rd year Information Technology student with a growing passion for UI & UX design. I enjoy designing interfaces that feel clean, friendly, and easy to understand.

My favorite part of the process is shaping the visual story: colors, spacing, typography, and small animations that make using a product feel delightful. I also enjoy translating designs into front‑end code using tools like Tailwind CSS.

Design principles

  • Simple & readable layouts
  • Soft gradients & pastel tones
  • Consistent spacing & rhythm
